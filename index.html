﻿PM to hold dialogue with political parties again: Quader

Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina will hold dialogue again with the political parties and alliances, which were invited at Gonobhaban before the general election, Awami League General Secretary Obaidul Quader has said. The Awami League leader told this to journalists after a meeting with the party's leaders of four districts at the AL office on Bangabandhu Avenue in Dhaka on Sunday.
